About the Creator

Kannan Murugapandian, a student at DPS International School, Singapore, and a passionate technologist dedicated to "Tech for Good". A competitive programmer, he is a Bronze Medalist at the National Olympiad in Informatics (NOI) 2024, a State Topper in the International Informatics Olympiad (iiO) 2024, and a Quarter Finalist in the National STEM Championship (NSTEMC) '25. His projects include DonumAI, an AI-powered teaching assistant, and founding Earth Status, a sustainability-focused organisation. Recognized for his technical proficiency with certifications like the CSARC Grade 2 in Arduino, he is continuing building innovative solutions for real-world challenges.
